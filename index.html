Wireless and Mobile computing

Wireless refers to a method of transferring information between a computing service such as a personal data assistant (PDA) without a physical connection. Not all wireless communication technology is mobile.

Mobile simply describes a computing device that is not restricted to a device. A mobile device may be a PDA a smart cell phone or a web phone a laptop computer or any one of the numerous other devices that allow the user to complete computing tasks without being connected to a network.
